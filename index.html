Executive Branch

President: Ranaús Shillé

This is the residential and official office of the President, where political and ceremonial duties are conducted. The white façade represents power and dignity.

Legislative Branch — Parliament (“The House”)

House Speaker: John Paige

Upper House: Senate

Lower House: House of the People

The bicameral system ensures balanced discussion and representation. The Senate refines legislation, while the House of the People votes on provincial representation.
